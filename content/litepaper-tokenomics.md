# Ryvra Litepaper: Tokenomics Snapshot

Ryvra applies Proof of Transaction (PoT) to align measurable participation with network growth.

## Core mechanics

- Pre-TGE phase tracks participation points for eligible transactions.
- Baseline accrual is capped at **2 points per day** per eligible account.
- Epoch emissions follow **proportional halving** to preserve long-term distribution discipline.
- Current roadmap references a **Q4 2026 TGE window**, subject to governance and legal review.

## Formulas

- `Daily Points = min(2, Eligible Activity Score × Epoch Weight)`
- `Epoch Weight(e) = 1 / 2^(e-1)`
- `Total Points = Σ Daily Points`
